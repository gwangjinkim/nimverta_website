Developing safe and effective long-term therapies for bone disease

Our Science

Age-related bone diseases ignore the crucial vascular environment and lack safe, long-term anabolic options. Nimverta's therapy regenerates not just the bone, but also the critical vascular network that supports it, leading to truly effective and long-lasting results.
